Kalahari Desert Expedition

6 Days / 5 Nights - Small Groups (Max 8) - From $1,850

Discover the Unique Ecosystem of the Kalahari

Venture into the vast expanse of the Kalahari Desert, home to specially adapted wildlife and ancient cultures. This expedition offers a rare opportunity to experience one of Africa's last true wilderness areas.

The Kalahari is not a true desert but a vast sand basin characterized by red sand dunes, sparse vegetation, and unique wildlife adapted to arid conditions. Here you'll encounter species like the majestic gemsbok, springbok, and the famous Kalahari lions.

Our Kalahari expedition goes beyond wildlife viewing to include cultural interactions with the San people, one of the world's oldest cultures. Learn about their ancient survival skills, tracking techniques, and deep connection to this harsh but beautiful landscape.

What's Included

  • Accommodation in remote desert camps
  • All meals and beverages (including water)
  • Expert guide with extensive Kalahari experience
  • Game drives in specialized safari vehicles
  • Cultural interactions with San Bushmen
  • Nature walks and tracking experiences
  • All park fees and permits

Safari Highlights

  • Encounter desert-adapted wildlife
  • Learn ancient tracking skills from San guides
  • Witness breathtaking Kalahari sunsets
  • Explore the iconic red sand dunes
  • Stargazing in incredibly clear skies
  • Visit Deception Valley and other iconic locations
  • Photograph unique desert landscapes
